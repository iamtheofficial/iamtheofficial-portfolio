About

Iamtheofficial

Reuben James, professionally known as Iamtheofficial, is a music producer, artist, and creative from Papakura, Auckland, with over 15 years of hands-on experience using FL Studio. His journey in music began at a young age and has developed into a versatile production style shaped by hip-hop, R&B, reggae, boom bap, soulful melodies, and West Coast G-funk influences.

Over the years, Iamtheofficial has worked with a wide range of local New Zealand artists, building a strong reputation for high-quality, genre-blending production. His approach combines technical skill, musical instinct, and a deep understanding of how to bring an artist’s vision to life.

Alongside his work with artists, Reuben has actively competed in producer battles and beat-making competitions. He placed 4th in Rep Your Beats 2021 and 2nd in Rep Your Beats 2023, hosted by Southside and RepFM. He also took 1st place in TheSoundProjekt 2022 Beatmaker Battle, hosted by TheProjektTeam. His competitive experience also extends internationally through online battles including The Gauntlet and Spells Beat Battles on Twitch.

Reuben has also been contracted by Serato to provide original compositions used to help train their AI stem-splitting algorithms, further reflecting the quality, clarity, and versatility of his production work.

With years of dedication behind his craft, Iamtheofficial continues to create music that is polished, adaptable, and emotionally driven. His work reflects a strong commitment to creativity, collaboration, and delivering productions that connect with both artists and listeners.
